Computer Science and Technology

...is what johnny is currently working hard to understand.

Johnny has been giving a lot of attention to understanding and leveraging digital technologies and past and future infrastructure. Johnny feels that technology has the potential for great advances and evolutionary self-actualization, but the opposite potential is a very real tangent which he feels will be all too easy to manifest, and if it happens, it will likely be due to ignorance, greed, and exploitation of those without resource and capital by those who control the majority of said resources.

Photography

...is one of Johnny's oldest passions.

Around the time he was in middle school, Johnny's interest in photography had emerged and he would shoot hundreds of rolls of film before he began working (not exclusively) with digitally formated cameras. The subjects he would be drawn to were primarily people, although landscapes, street scenes, and more contextual compositions found their place in the frame. Self-taught, Johnny never pursued commercial success in the professional photographic arena and he has tended to focus on his personally driven vision. You can find most of his work under the alias, Johnny Shot Me.

Education

...is the career in which johnny has spent the past 13 years.

Johnny has been teaching since he left college in the Winter of 2007. Over the past fourteen years, Johnny has taught in South Korea, Ecuador, El Paso, and New York, the latter making up the majority of his educational career. Currently, Johnny teaches technology in a school that is comprised of students with special needs in Brooklyn, New York. His pedagogical peity is welded to the belief that the teacher's purpose is to guide students to proficiently feed their own thirst for understanding through a consistently critical, contextual, and pragmatic lens.
